My name is Max Feliu and I am a student at UNC-Chapel Hill, from Barcelona, Spain. I have lived in Spain, England, and in the United States before starting college. In my experiences in the different countries I have lived in, I have developed a passion for different sports entities around the world. My passions of written and video reporting have led me to a career in Media and Journalism . I currently work as a sports writer for The Daily Tar Heel and I have experience in sports broadcast control rooms. With my work at the DTH along with personal projects I have undertaken, I have gotten to to publish content in a while expanding my skills as a journalist.
